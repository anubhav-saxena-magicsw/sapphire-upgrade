Newton?s 3rd Law A sprinter applies large forces into the ground (ground reaction forces) to cause an equal and opposite force, propelling their body in a forwards direction. Newton?s?3rd Law also explains the transfer of momentum from one object to another during impact situations in sport. The object with the largest momentum (mass x velocity) will transfer some of their force onto the other object.

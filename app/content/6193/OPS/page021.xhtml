Impulse The amount of force which can be used for movement increases when it is applied over a longer period of time. This principle of force-time is known as impulse is also used to describe change in momentum. Forces can applied over a longer period of time by increasing the amount of time of preparation, action and follow through phases. Often this involves positioning as many body parts behind the intended direction of movement as possible and directing them through the movement towards the target. Impulse Force ?

Components of Levers A lever is a rigid bar which rotates around a fixed axis. Levers are used to produce force and velocity of movement more efficiently and are divided into 1st, 2nd and 3rd class levers according to the way the axis, force arm and resistance arm are configured. The human body is made up of mainly third class levers with the force arm length being shorter than the resistance arm length and both being on the same side of the lever axis.

Positioning line of gravity for quick movement Positioning line of gravity towards an edge of the base of support creates instability and allows a person to move quickly in that direction with a small amount of force. Example: Body positioning in sprint start Sprinter moves their centre of gravity towards the forward edge of the base of support. This creates relative instability enabling them to move in a forwards direction very quickly. Sprinters adjust position of starting blocks to maximise ground reaction forces according to lever length.

Co-efficient of Restitution When two pieces of sporting equipment collide (such as a bat striking a ball) some deformation occurs. The type of material each object is made of will determine its elasticity and ability to return to its original shape after the collision. Co-efficient of Restitution (e) is a measure of an objects elasticity and determines the size of an objects rebound after collision has occurred.

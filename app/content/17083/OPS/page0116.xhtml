Read the information below about stem cells, and then complete the Learned column of your KWL.ReadingSeveral Types of Stem CellsIn the previous activity, you learned that stem cells often differentiate through multiple cell cycles to produce a variety of types of specialized cells. A human fer-tilized egg, or zygote, can produce cells that differentiate into every kind of spe-cialized human cell. This means the zygote is totipotent, totally capable. When the zygote ﬁrst begins dividing, its cells form a hollow ball, called a blastocyst.

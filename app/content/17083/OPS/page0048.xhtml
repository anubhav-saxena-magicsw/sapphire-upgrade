SOME TIPS FOR BETTER DRAWINGS: • Use a sharp pencil and have a good eraser available. • Try to relax your eyes when looking through the eyepiece. You can cover one eye or learn to look with both eyes open. Try not to squint. • Look through your microscope at the same time as you do your drawing. Look through the microscope more than you look at your paper.• Don’t draw every small thing on your slide. Just concentrate on one or two of the most common or interesting things. • You can draw things larger than you actually see them. This helps you show all of the details you see. • Keep written words outside the circle.• Use a ruler to draw the lines for your labels.

LITERACY STRATEGIES521Read, Think, and Take Note Reading StrategyAs you read, from time to time, write one of the following on a sticky note:• Explain a thought or reaction to something you read.• Note something in the reading that is confusing or unfamiliar.• List a word that you do not know.• Describe a connection to something you learned or read previously.• Make a statement about the reading.• Pose a question about the reading.• Draw a diagram or picture of an idea or connection.After writing the sticky note, place it next to the word, phrase, sentence, or paragraph in the reading that prompted your note.

EASUREMENTS THAT APPEAR in Science and Global Issues are expressed in metric units from the International System of Units, otherwise known as SI units (from Système Internationale d’Unités), which was established by inter-national agreement. Virtually all countries in the world mandate use of the metric system exclusively. e United States does not use the metric system for many measurements, although it has been the standard for the scientiﬁc community in the United States for more than 200 years.

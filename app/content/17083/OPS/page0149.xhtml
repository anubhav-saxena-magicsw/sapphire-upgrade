SCIENCE & GLOBAL ISSUES/BIOLOGY • APPENDIX B528Scatterplot and Line Graphing ChecklistSample GraphFollow the instructions below to make a sample line graph. Start with a table of data. Determine whether a line graph or a scatterplot is the best way to represent the data. Draw the axes. Label them with the names and units of the data. Decide on a scale for each axis. Be sure there is enough space for all the data, but that it’s not too crowded.

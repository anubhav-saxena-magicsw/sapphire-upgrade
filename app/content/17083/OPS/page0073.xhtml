The Cell Membrane and Diffusion
A
KEY FUNCTION OF the cell membrane is regulating what sub-stances enter or leave the cell. In many cases, substances move into or out of the cell through the process of diffusion. In diffusion, a substance moves from a solution with a high concen-tration of the substance to a solution with a low concentration of the substance.

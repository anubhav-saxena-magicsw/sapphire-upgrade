STUDY GUIDE B6:
INVENTORIES

GET THROUGH INTRO

For manufacturing and trading entities, inventories are the main component of current assets and the working capital cycle. They generate operating profits. Efficient management and reporting of inventories are essential for the correct calculation of profits or losses.

Incorrect figures for inventory can lead to reporting incorrect profits or losses as well as current assets. This may mislead users. Management, taxation authorities, lenders and investors may take wrong decisions if they base their decisions on the wrong figures for profits or losses.

You will have to provide reliable reports to a range of decision-makers in the future. Therefore, you need to know the correct principles of accounting for inventories.

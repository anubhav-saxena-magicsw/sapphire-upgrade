STUDY GUIDE E1:
PRINCIPAL DIFFERENCES BETWEEN UK/US GAAP AND IFRS

GET THROUGH INTRO

An important part of achieving Cert IFR qualification, is keeping up to date with current issues. This is becoming increasingly important as standards are changing and new issues are being discussed.

If you are to remain at the forefront of your profession, you will need to keep abreast of current issues in accounting and finance. This is easy to do when you are studying - we do it for you, by updating the books! However, once you are qualified, you will need to take the lead.

This Study Guide introduces you to some of the topics that you should know are creating waves within financial reporting and accounting.

Remember, you should be the one to tell your boss of the latest changes, or at least be able to discuss the issues with him / her. That is how you can truly show your value in the work place.

This Study Guide highlights the principal differences between IFRS and UK/US GAAP.

LEARNING OUTCOMES

a) Proposed changes in IFRS.

b) Principal differences between IFRS and UK GAAP.

c) Principal differences between IFRS and US GAAP.

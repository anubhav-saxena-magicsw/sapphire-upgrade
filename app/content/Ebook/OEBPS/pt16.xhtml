Elements of Financial Statements

STUDY GUIDE B15:
INSURANCE CONTRACTS

GET THROUGH INTRO

The uncertainty resulting from the underlying risks that an insurance entity is exposed to is the key issue in relation to insurance contracts. Hence, it was important to have a framework which sets out common principles to provide relevant information in order to enhance the user’s understanding of the entity’s financial statements. IFRS 4 was framed with this purpose.
